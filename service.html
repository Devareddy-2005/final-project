Services offered by our watch shop:

1. Sales of Watches:


- Luxury Watches: High-end brands such as Rolex, Omega, Patek Philippe, and Breitling.
- Mid-Range Watches: Brands like Seiko, Tissot, and Citizen.
- Fashion Watches: Brands like Michael Kors, Fossil, and Guess.
- Smartwatches: Brands like Apple, Samsung, and Garmin.

2. Watch Repair Services:

- Battery Replacement: Replacing watch batteries.
- Strap Replacement: Changing and fitting new watch straps or bracelets.
- Movement Repair: Fixing the internal mechanisms of the watch.
- Crystal Replacement: Replacing scratched or cracked watch crystals.
- Water Resistance Testing: Ensuring watches are properly sealed against water.
- Polishing and Cleaning: Restoring the watch's appearance by cleaning and polishing.

3. Watch Customization:

- Engraving: Personalizing the watch with engravings.
- Custom Dials and Hands: Modifying the watch face to fit personal styles.
- Custom Straps: Creating bespoke straps from various materials like leather, metal, or rubber.

4. Watch Appraisal and Authentication:

- Valuation Services: Providing appraisals for insurance or resale purposes.
- Authentication: Verifying the authenticity of watches, especially for high-end brands.

5. Watch Trade-In and Buyback Programs:

- Trade-In Services: Allowing customers to trade in old watches for credit towards a new purchase.
- Buyback Programs: Offering to buy watches back from customers.

6. Consignment Services:

- Selling on Behalf of Customers: Selling watches on consignment, where the shop sells the watch and takes a commission.

7. Watch Maintenance Plans:

- Regular Maintenance Packages: Offering packages for regular servicing and maintenance of watches.

8. Specialized Services for Collectors:

- Collector Consultations: Offering advice and expertise for watch collectors.
- Limited Editions: Selling exclusive or limited-edition watches.

9. Online Services:

- E-Commerce: Selling watches online through a dedicated e-commerce platform.
- Virtual Consultations: Providing virtual consultations and advice for purchasing or maintaining watches.

10. Educational Workshops and Events:

- Watchmaking Classes: Offering classes on the basics of watchmaking and maintenance.
- Collector Events: Hosting events for watch enthusiasts and collectors to meet and discuss.

11. Warranty Services:

- Warranty Repairs: Handling warranty repairs and replacements.
- Extended Warranties: Offering extended warranty options for new purchases.
These services can help cater to a wide range of customer needs, from casual buyers to serious collectors.
